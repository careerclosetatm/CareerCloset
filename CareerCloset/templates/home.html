How the Texas A&M Career Closet Works

Setup an Appointment

Visit the "APPOINTMENT" page to book an appointment online. Also, be sure to visit the "AVAILABILITY" page to ensure that the attire is indeed available at the time of scheduling. Visit the Texas A&M University Career Closet in person at MSC Room L428 at the time of your appointment. You will be able to create a date and time to try on and pick up your business attire.

Choose your Business Attire

During your appointment, you will be able to try on different pieces of business attire to find a size and style that suits you. You will have five days to use the business attire for important interviews, networking events, and other occasions where you want to make a strong first impression. Under certain circumstances, the rental period can be extended to accommodate students who may need to rent business attire for periods longer than five days.

Dry Clean & Return

Before you return the business attire, you must take it by Park Cleaners (located at 314 George Bush Dr. College Station, Texas 77840) and pay the $5 fee to have the attire dry cleaned. All you will need to do after that is return the attire to the MSC Room L428 during normal business hours.
